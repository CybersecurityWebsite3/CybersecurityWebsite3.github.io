FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

What exactly is cybercrime?

Cybercrime involves illegal activities carried out using computers or the internet, unlike traditional crime.

What are the most common types of cybercrime?

Common types include hacking, identity theft, phishing, ransomware, and online fraud.

How can individuals protect themselves from becoming victims of cybercrime?

Use strong passwords, enable two-factor authentication, update software regularly, and be cautious of suspicious emails or links.

What role do hackers play in cybercrime?

Hackers exploit security vulnerabilities in systems to steal data, cause damage, or demand ransom.

What are the legal consequences of committing cybercrime?

Consequences can include fines, imprisonment, or both, depending on the severity of the crime and local laws.
